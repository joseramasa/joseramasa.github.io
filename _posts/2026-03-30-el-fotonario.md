I'm happy to share [El Fotonario](https://joseramasa.github.io/fotonica/), a project I've been building to make photonics accessible in Spanish.

It's an interactive educational platform covering optics from the ground up: electromagnetic foundations, Fourier optics, fiber optics, lasers, integrated photonics, and plasmonics. It includes 9 modular courses, 145 exercises with feedback, 78 interactive visualizations, and a 458-term glossary.

### Why?

Most quality resources in photonics are in English. That's fine for researchers, but it creates a barrier for undergraduate students and professionals across Latin America and Spain who would benefit from learning these concepts in their native language. The goal is simple: remove that barrier.

### What's next

The platform will keep growing with new modules, more exercises, and community contributions. If you teach optics in Spanish and want to collaborate, reach out.
