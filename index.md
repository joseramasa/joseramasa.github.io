I am **José Ramón Martínez Saavedra**, VP of Innovation and Lead Scientist at [Quside Technologies](https://quside.com), where I lead research and technology transfer in quantum random number generation, photonics, and high-performance computing for cybersecurity.

I hold a **Ph.D. in Photonics** (Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya / ICFO, 2018, *Cum Laude*) under the supervision of Prof. F. Javier García de Abajo, and an **MBA** from Valar Institute (2025). My academic background includes a degree in Physics from Universidad Complutense de Madrid and an MSc in Photonics from UPC/UAB/UB/ICFO.

My current research interests span **quantum technologies**, **randomness generation and certification**, **post-quantum cryptography**, and **technology transfer** from fundamental research to market-ready products. I am Principal Investigator of the Horizon Europe project [RPU](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101145131) and the CDTI project PICNET.

I also teach *Software Architecture for Quantum Computers* at UPC's [Postgraduate Course in Quantum Engineering](https://www.talent.upc.edu/ing/estudis/formacio/curs/331400/postgrau-quantum-engineering/), and supervise doctoral and master's theses at UPC and ICFO.
