I have contributed chapters to three collaborative popular science books in Spanish, all coordinated by Quintín Garrido and published under Creative Commons. Each book brings together dozens of scientists writing about their fields in an accessible way.

### Ciencia, y además lo entiendo

Chapters 62 & 63. A book structured around questions across mathematics, physics, astrophysics, chemistry, biology, neuroscience, geology, and materials science.

[Download (free PDF)](https://divulgacioncientificadecientificos.blogspot.com/p/libro-book.html)

### Ciencia, y yo quiero ser científico

Chapter 70. Over 100 STEM professionals share their experiences and career paths to help young people (14–18) discover what kind of scientist they want to be.

[Download (free PDF)](https://cienciayyoquierosercientifico.blogspot.com/)

### Ciencia y otras easy pieces

Chapter 37. A tribute to Feynman's Lectures on Physics, with over 50 scientists revisiting the lessons that shaped modern physics — 60 years later.

[Download (free PDF)](https://cienciayotraseasypieces.blogspot.com/)
