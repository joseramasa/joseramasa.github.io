**[El Fotonario — Óptica interactiva en español](https://joseramasa.github.io/fotonica/)**

An interactive, open educational resource covering photonics and optics in Spanish. From Fourier optics to plasmonics, through fiber optics and integrated photonics.

### What's inside

- **9 modular courses** progressing from electromagnetic fundamentals to nanophotonics
- **145 interactive exercises** with immediate feedback
- **78 visualizations** of optical phenomena
- **458-term glossary** in Spanish

### Topics covered

1. Electromagnetic foundations
2. Fourier optics
3. Numerical simulation methods
4. Lasers and light sources
5. Waveguides and fiber optics
6. Optical devices
7. Integrated photonics
8. Plasmonics and nanophotonics
9. Advanced topics

Built as a free resource for Spanish-speaking students and professionals in photonics.
