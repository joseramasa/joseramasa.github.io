**[Con la Venia — Derecho Procesal Civil](https://joseramasa.github.io/con-la-venia/)**

An interactive visual novel for learning Civil Procedural Law (*Derecho Procesal Civil*), built with the Ren'Py engine and deployed as a web application.

### Concept

Learning law doesn't have to be dry. *Con la Venia* uses scenario-based storytelling to walk the player through civil procedural law — from filing a claim to trial and appeal — making legal concepts tangible through narrative choices and consequences.

### Features

- Interactive narrative with branching decisions
- Covers Spanish civil procedural law fundamentals
- Save/load progress, export/import saves
- Offline support via service worker
- Built with Ren'Py, deployed on GitHub Pages

A personal project at the intersection of technology, education, and my ongoing Law studies at UOC.
